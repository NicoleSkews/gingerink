Ginger Ink is a boutique communications service started by Nicole Skews-Poole.

Nicole is an experienced communications all-rounder and leads Ginger Ink with skills ranging from the management of nationwide online campaigns to internal communications. She has experience in government, start up, social services and financial sectors and brings a passion for accessible, audience-focussed messaging to her work.

Nicole works remotely from Wellington, New Zealand.

As part of your project we can also put you in contact with web developers, designers and illustrators. Just contact us and ask.
